Research Interests

  • AI Safety: My recent work focuses on advanced LLM moderation. I'm developing methods that move beyond simple binary classification (safe/unsafe) to precisely remove harmful information while preserving the utility of the response.
  • Interpretability: I've explored methods (e.g., Activation Steering, Sparse Autoencoder) to gain insights into the internal mechanisms of LLMs. Beyond simply controlling these models without understanding their inner workings, I aim to comprehend how LLMs make decisions and leverage world knowledge to perform complex tasks.
